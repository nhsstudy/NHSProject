Total Resident COVID-19 Case Trends between 2020 and 2021

Tips for use:

1. Select the State that you would like to view through the use of the "Provider State" filter.
2.Once the state is set, you can select a particular city that you would like to view through the "Provider City" filter.
3. You can adjust which providers that you would like to see on the graph by unchecking or checking the boxes in the "Provider Name" filter. Additionally, you can hover over the lines on the graph to view things such as the provider name, year, Total # of cases, etc.
